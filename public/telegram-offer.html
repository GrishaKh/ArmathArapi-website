The concept

One hub, two spaces

A single welcoming front door for the whole community, plus a focused workspace for the people doing the work — held together by one friendly bot.

The public lobby

Anyone can join. Announcements, events, and open Q&A. This is where your 300+ users gather.

The member zone

A private space for the verified 75 + staff, with a dedicated thread for each village to coordinate real work.

The helper bot

The front desk: welcomes people, verifies members, serves resources, and keeps everyone in sync.

The member journey

From newcomer to member

1

Join the lobby

A QR poster or link drops them into the public lobby. They can read along right away.

2

Open the bot

One tap on Start → choose language (Հայ / Eng) → choose their village.

3

Enter the code

Selected members get a one-time code at selection. The bot checks it — no phone numbers needed.

4

Step inside

Valid code → a single-use invite to the member zone and their village thread. Done.

No code? No problem — they stay a welcome guest in the lobby with full access to resources, events and the FAQ, and can apply through the bot or the Google Form.

Capabilities

What the bot does

01

Onboarding & verification

Greets joiners, asks village, verifies the 75 by code, assigns roles, and moves members across to the private zone.

02

Moderation & safety

Captcha on join, anti-spam, welcome & pinned rules, mute / ban helpers — keeps a 300+ space healthy with a small team.

03

Content & info

Serves your life-hack guides and PDFs on demand, answers FAQs, and collects “urgent problem in my village” reports.

04

Comms & events

Staff post once → the bot fans out and pins. Event lists, RSVPs, and countdown reminders (e.g. the application deadline).

Why this approach

Built to last, easy to measure

  • Always-on & reliable — no sleeping free tiers; the hub is up when the community is.
  • Low running cost — from €0 to a few euros a month.
  • On-brand & Armenian-first — matches the LAG’s identity and language.
  • Privacy-friendly verification — one-time codes, no phone-number harvesting.
  • You own it — the data and the code belong to the LAG.
