The Problem

Why this matters now

No Armenian-Language STEM Content

Most quality STEM educational materials are only available in foreign languages, creating a language barrier that limits students' access to technology education.

Lack of Hands-On STEM Kits

Both formal and non-formal institutions face a severe shortage of comprehensive, project-based Armenian-language learning packages.

Declining Student Interest in STEM

Research shows project-based learning (PBL) develops real skills, but the lack of affordable tools causes interest to drop from an early age, especially in rural areas.

Environmental Data Gap

Air pollution is an invisible health threat. Akhuryan community lacks the data and awareness tools needed to monitor and address environmental issues proactively.

Our Solution

EduKit: Learn by Building

An Eco-STEM Educational Kit that creates real impact

Students learn programming and electronics by building their own air quality monitoring device. The kit includes Armenian-language tutorials, electronic components, and connects to an open-source data platform.

  • Armenian-language practical guides
  • Electronic components & sensors (PM2.5)
  • Open-source centralized data platform
  • Project-based learning methodology
  • Citizen Science data collection model
